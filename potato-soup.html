Jamie's Loaded Potato Soup

Description

This soup is delightful and perfect for a cold blustery day. It is at its most delicious when cooked over a roaring fire in a wood stove in a cast iron dutch oven, but a regular soup pot will do!

Ingredients

  • 5 lbs russet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 lb bacon
  • 8 oz sour cream
  • 3/4 cup salted butter
  • 1 large Vidalia onion, diced
  • 1/2 cup white flour
  • 2 32 oz. containers of chicken broth

Instructions

  1. Cook bacon in skillet until crispy. Remove from skillet and dice. Leave bacon grease in skillet.
  2. Add onions to skillet and cook until tender in bacon grease, 5 minutes or so.
  3. While onions are cooking, add chicken broth and potatoes to large cookpot or dutch oven. Bring to boil and then simmer for 20 minutes or until potatoes are soft.
  4. When onions are tender and potatoes are done, add onions and grease to cookpot with potatoes.
  5. Use immersion blender to whip potatoes and onions into creamy liquid in cookpot with broth.
